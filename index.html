Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our service.

Is the registration fee refundable?

No, the one-time registration fee is non-refundable. This helps maintain the exclusivity and quality of our service.

How often do you send insights?

We focus on quality over quantity. Insights are sent daily, typically ranging from 1 to 3 picks per day, based on our confidence level and market value.

Can I share my access with others?

No, membership is strictly non-transferable. Sharing credentials compromises the security of our private channels and may result in account termination.

What happens if I miss a pick?

All insights are archived within our Telegram group history. You can review past messages at any time. We also send reminders before important matches.

Do you offer free trials or previews?

No, we do not offer free trials or previews after registration. Our service is built on trust and exclusivity, which requires a commitment from our members.

What types of predictions do you offer?

We provide a variety of predictions including match winner, total goals (Over/Under), both teams to score (BTTS), and correct score predictions, depending on the match and our analysis confidence.

How do you calculate your success rate?

Our success rate is calculated based on the total number of successful predictions divided by the total number of predictions made, multiplied by 100. A 'successful' prediction means the outcome matched our insight.

Who is Cool Kid?

Cool Kid is the founder and lead analyst of CoolKid Tips. He has over 10 years of experience in sports analytics and has built this service from the ground up with a focus on accuracy and member satisfaction.

Do you guarantee profits?

No, we cannot guarantee profits. All sports betting involves risk. Our service provides informed analysis to help you make better decisions, but outcomes are never certain.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team via WhatsApp (+254 113 206503), Telegram (@coolkidtips), or email (info@coolkidtips.com).
